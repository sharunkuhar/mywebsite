---
title: 'Duodenogastric Reflux in Health and Disease: Insights from a CFD Model'
description: 'Our new paper provides novel insights into the mechanisms of duodenogastric reflux using a computational fluid dynamics model of the stomach.'
pubDate: 'Mar 28 2025'
---

Normally, the contents of the stomach empty into the intestines. But, in some scenarios, they can occasionally flow back via unclear mechanisms leading to a plethora of complications.

We provide novel insights into the phenomenon of duodenogastric reflux in our recent publication:

Sharun Kuhar, Jung Hee Seo, Pankaj Jay Pasricha, Michael Camilleri, and Rajat Mittal. "Duodenogastric Reflux in Health and Disease: Insights from a Computational Fluid Dynamics Model of the Stomach." *American Journal of Physiology-Gastrointestinal and Liver Physiology*, p. ajpgi.00241.2024, Jan. 2025. [https://doi.org/10.1152/ajpgi.00241.2024](https://doi.org/10.1152/ajpgi.00241.2024)
