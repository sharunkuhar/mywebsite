We are organizing a mini-symposium titled "Computational Modeling of Physiological Flows," at the 2026 Society of Engineering Science (SES) Annual Technical Meeting, to be held at Purdue University, West Lafayette, Indiana on October 11th–14th, 2026.

If you are interested, abstract submission is now open and will close on May 1st, 2026. Please see the mini-symposia and submission instructions on the conference website: [https://lnkd.in/ggN3eVRk](https://lnkd.in/ggN3eVRk)

We'll be grateful if you could also share this information with students or colleagues who might be interested. If you have any questions, please do not hesitate to contact us.

— Sharun Kuhar, Dongjie Jia, Arezoo Ardekani

Conference website: [https://socengsci.org/](https://socengsci.org/)
