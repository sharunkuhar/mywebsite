title: 'In Silico Modelling of Pyloric Intervention Procedures in Gastroparesis'
description: 'High-fidelity CFD models of the stomach investigate the effects of gastric surgery in gastroparesis patients, validated against in vivo measurements.'
pubDate: 'Feb 21 2024'
---

Next in our series of high-fidelity computational fluid dynamics models of the stomach, we investigated the effects of gastric surgery in different gastroparesis patients. We compare our model against in-vivo measurements and present novel explanations for why these surgeries are not always effective.

Sharun Kuhar, Jung Hee Seo, Pankaj Jay Pasricha, and Rajat Mittal. "In Silico Modelling of the Effect of Pyloric Intervention Procedures on Gastric Flow and Emptying in a Stomach with Gastroparesis." *Journal of The Royal Society Interface* 21, no. 210 (January 2024): 20230567. [https://doi.org/10.1098/rsif.2023.0567](https://doi.org/10.1098/rsif.2023.0567)
