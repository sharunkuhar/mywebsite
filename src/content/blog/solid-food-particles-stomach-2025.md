---
title: 'In Silico Study of Solid Food Particles in the Stomach During Gastric Digestion'
description: 'The first computational model of its kind incorporating large solid food particles to study how the stomach breaks them down during digestion.'
pubDate: 'Sep 09 2025'
---

Assuming contents to be purely liquid is a common sin committed by modern computational models of the stomach. In our new publication, we present the first of its kind model that incorporates large solid food particles. Read if interested in how our stomach breaks down large solid food particles:

Sharun Kuhar, Jung Hee Seo, and Rajat Mittal. "In silico study of the dynamics of solid food particles in the stomach during gastric digestion." *Journal of the Royal Society Interface* 22.229 (2025): 20250291. [https://doi.org/10.1098/rsif.2025.0291](https://doi.org/10.1098/rsif.2025.0291)
