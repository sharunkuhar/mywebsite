It was an honor to be invited to the White House yesterday for a gathering of the inaugural Quad Fellows and meet the Principal Deputy National Security Advisor Jon Finer along with other senior administration officials and representatives from the Indian, Japanese, and Australian Embassies.

Read the full official briefing here: [https://www.whitehouse.gov/briefing-room/statements-releases/2024/01/30/readout-of-the-quad-stem-fellows-event-at-the-white-house/](https://www.whitehouse.gov/briefing-room/statements-releases/2024/01/30/readout-of-the-quad-stem-fellows-event-at-the-white-house/)

The applications for the next cohort start tomorrow. For all those who reached out to me over the past year for applications: [quadfellowship.org](https://www.quadfellowship.org).
