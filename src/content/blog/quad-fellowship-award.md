I'm excited to announce that I was awarded the Quad Fellowship!

This fellowship was launched this year by the governments of Australia, India, Japan, and the USA, to select 100 graduate students — 25 from each Quad country — who demonstrate:

1. Academic excellence in STEM
2. Passion for the intersection of science, society, and policy
3. Capacity to bridge differences
4. Orientation toward results

Read more about it here: [https://www.quadfellowship.org/](https://www.quadfellowship.org/)

A speech by PM Narendra Modi from its launch: [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OS4ygubOP1o](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=OS4ygubOP1o)

Thanks to all my professors for their support. I look forward to the residential and virtual programs that are part of this fellowship.
